ABOUT ME

My name is Ian Miller and I am a junior full stack web developer based in Los Angeles, California USA. My coding journey began back in 2018 while attending the UCLA coding bootcamp, and earning my certificate with an A. The comprehensive course led me to master HTML, CSS, JavsScript/JQuery, Node.JS/Express, React, MySQL, and MongoDB. Not only did it teach me how to code, but it taught me how to work as effectively and efficiently as possible when it came to group projects, with methods like daily SCRUM meetings and project boards to keep track of the project's progress. I have gone on to complete a contracted position for the City of Los Angeles, where I developed an MVC web application using CakePHP and MySQL and also aided in the development of an ASP.Net application related to the project. My focus and passion is building complex logic in JavaScript.

I am a disciplined developer with a strong work ethic and a good team player no matter the situation. I am a quick learner and I use this skill to attain any knowledge I need to get the job done. My main career goal is gain enough experience to become a senior developer leading groups for projects at major tech companies. In my down time, I am bringing some of my app ideas to fruition to hone my skills. Other hobbies outside of coding are hiking, exercising, and gaming. If you have any interest in collaborating, please feel free to contact me at any time!
