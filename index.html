ABOUT ME

My name is Ian Miller and I am a full stack web developer based in Los Angeles, California USA. My coding journey back in 2018 while attending the UCLA coding bootcamp and earning my certificate with an A. The comprehensive course led me to master HTML, CSS, JavsScript/JQuery, Node.JS/Express, React, MySQL, and MongoDB. I have gone on to complete a contracted position for the City of Los Angeles and various free-lance gigs. My focus and passion is building complex logic in JavaScript.
